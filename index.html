Introduction

World War II was one of the largest and deadliest wars in history. It started in 1939 and ended in 1945. The war involved many countries around the world and caused major political, economic, and social changes.

Main Causes of the War

  • Treaty of Versailles after World War I
  • Rise of Adolf Hitler in Germany
  • Economic crises in Europe
  • Expansion of Nazi Germany

Important Events

Some of the most important events during World War II include:

  1. Germany invaded Poland in 1939
  2. Attack on Pearl Harbor in 1941
  3. D-Day invasion in 1944
  4. The war ended in 1945

Impact of the War

The war caused millions of deaths and destruction across many countries. It also led to the creation of the United Nations and major technological developments.
